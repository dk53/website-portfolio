Features

Made for Audiobooks

🎧

AirPods Controls

Tap to skip forward, double-tap to go back, press to play or pause. Works from the lock screen too.

🔊

Background Playback

Keep listening while you browse, message, or lock your phone. Full lock screen and Control Center integration.

📖

M4B Chapters

Native chapter support for M4B files and multi-file audiobooks. Navigate hours of content effortlessly.

⏱️

Sleep Timer

Set a timer and fall asleep to your book. Smooth fade-out so you never miss where you stopped.

📍

Never Lose Your Place

Position is saved automatically, always. Close the app, restart your phone, come back days later. You're right where you left off.

📁

Import From Anywhere

Files app, iCloud Drive, Google Drive, Dropbox. Bring your audiobooks from any source.

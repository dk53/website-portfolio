Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 7, 2026

Audiobook Reader ("the App") is developed by Victor Carmouze. This policy describes how the App handles your data. The short version: your data stays on your device, and we don't collect anything.

Data Collection

Audiobook Reader does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App runs entirely on your device.

Specifically, the App does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data
  • Crash reports or diagnostics

Audio Files

Audio files you import into the App are stored locally on your device. The App accesses files only through Apple's standard file picker (Document Picker) and security-scoped bookmarks. Files are never uploaded to any server.

Playback Data

Your library, playback positions, bookmarks, and preferences are stored locally on your device in the App's sandbox. This data is never transmitted off your device.

Third-Party Services

Audiobook Reader does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. There are no SDKs from Facebook, Google Analytics, Firebase, or similar platforms.

Network Access

The App may access the network only when you import files from cloud storage providers (iCloud Drive, Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.) through Apple's standard file picker. These connections are managed entirely by iOS and the respective cloud provider apps. Audiobook Reader does not make any network requests itself.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children or anyone else. Since no data is collected, the App is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Since the App collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.
